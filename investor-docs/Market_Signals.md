# Market Signals & Comparable Momentum (Sources)

## AI coding assistants and builders
- GitHub Copilot surpassed **20M all-time users**; **90% of the Fortune 100** uses Copilot; enterprise adoption grew **~75% QoQ**. (TechCrunch, Jul 30 2025)
- Cursor reportedly exceeded **1M daily users** in March 2025 and scaled from **~$200M ARR** to **>$500M ARR** within months. (TechCrunch citing Bloomberg, Jul 2025)
- Enterprise AI coding tools are scaling rapidly, indicating strong willingness to pay for developer productivity.

## Customer service and voice AI adoption
- **85%** of customer service leaders will **explore or pilot** customer-facing conversational GenAI in 2025; **44%** are exploring a GenAI voicebot, **11%** are piloting, **5%** deployed. (Gartner, Dec 9 2024)
- **96%** of companies say AI is improving customer-facing operations; **75%** see increased customer spend from personalization; only **45%** of consumers feel understood. (Twilio State of Customer Engagement, Jun 3 2025)

## Market size (call-center AI)
- Global call center AI market size: **$1.99B in 2024**, projected **$7.08B by 2030** (**23.8% CAGR**). (Grand View Research, 2024 report)
